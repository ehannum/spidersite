Announcing our new game: Flesh It Out!

Flesh It Out is a creative party game of simultaneous action and monster mashing. You and up to 6 friends fight over a limited pile of parts in an effort to flesh out concepts from "Horse" to "Helicopter." Can't draw? You're in luck—all the art is done for you! Sure, your airplane may have to use legs for wings and eyes for wheels but beggars can't be choosers, Marcus! Use your imagination!

Want to help us out? We are always accepting suggestions for improvements, parts, concept cards, or really anything else. Also maybe follow us on Facebook and Twitter and stuff!
